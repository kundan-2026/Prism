About Prism Healthcare

Prism Healthcare and Rehabilitation Center is Bihar's premier destination for specialized physical therapy. We bridge the gap between acute medical treatment and functional independence by providing world-class rehabilitation standards locally.

Our units in Begusarai and Patna are equipped with advanced therapeutic modalities, including IASTM, Dry Needling, and specialized Neuro-Rehab equipment, ensuring that every patient receives the same level of care found in global metro-hospitals.

The Director

Dr. Ravi Kumar
BPT, MPT (Neurology), MIAP, CONT, CMTP

Dr. Ravi Kumar is a distinguished Neurological Physiotherapist with over a decade of clinical excellence.

Formerly associated with Yatharth Hospital and other premier medical institutions, he specializes in managing complex stroke recovery, spinal cord injuries, and advanced neurological dysfunction.

His clinical approach is deeply rooted in the latest global research and Evidence-Based Practice. He is a certified specialist in Manual Therapy (CONT/CMTP), allowing him to combine deep neurological understanding with advanced musculoskeletal manipulation techniques.

At Prism Healthcare, Dr. Kumar has pioneered specific protocols for NDT (Neuro-Developmental Treatment) and PNF, focusing on functional milestones. His vision is to provide metropolitan-standard rehabilitation care locally in Bihar, bridging the gap between hospital discharge and a patient's return to active life.

Whether it's chronic spine management or high-intensity post-surgical rehab, Dr. Ravi's clinical reasoning ensures that every patient receives a unique recovery blueprint rather than a generic exercise plan.

The Medical Team

Dr. Supriya BPT, Consultant Physiotherapist

Dr. Supriya is an integral part of our rehabilitation clinical board, specializing in Pediatric Physiotherapy and Post-Surgical recovery.

Her evidence-based approach helps patients achieve their functional milestones with precision, particularly in complex orthopedic post-operative cases.

She has extensive experience in Pediatric Neuro-rehabilitation, working with children to manage Cerebral Palsy and delayed developmental milestones. Her patient-friendly approach makes therapy engaging and effective for younger age groups.

Additionally, Dr. Supriya manages high-precision recovery for TKR (Total Knee Replacement) and THR (Total Hip Replacement) patients, focusing on early mobilization and gait training to ensure a fast return to daily activities.

The Medical Team

Dr. Puja BPT, Consultant Physiotherapist

Dr. Puja brings vast clinical expertise to Prism Healthcare, specializing in Women's Health and Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation.

She is dedicated to providing compassionate care and personalized exercise protocols for chronic pain management, ensuring every patient feels supported throughout their journey.

Her expertise extends to specialized Pre and Post-natal physiotherapy, helping women navigate physical changes with strength and comfort. She also focuses on Ergonomic corrections for corporate professionals facing repetitive strain injuries.

Dr. Puja's therapeutic philosophy emphasizes "Movement as Medicine," where she combines hands-on manual techniques with active functional training to prevent recurrence of injury.
